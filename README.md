# 🛡️ Aegis Cyber Solutions

> Digital Security • Account Recovery • Online Protection • Cyber Awareness

Aegis Cyber Solutions is a professional cybersecurity-focused web platform designed to provide individuals and organizations with guidance, support, and structured services for digital security, account recovery, and online threat response.

🌐 **Live Website:**  
https://aegis-cyber-solutions.vercel.app/

---

## 🚀 Overview

Aegis Cyber Solutions is built as a modern, responsive web application that focuses on:

- Cybersecurity awareness
- Ethical digital support services
- Online identity protection
- Account recovery assistance
- Safe promotion and reputation management

The platform is designed to simulate a **real-world cybersecurity service firm**, with structured workflows, client interaction points, and professional UI/UX.
